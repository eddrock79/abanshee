⟳ Slideshow

Tick the boards to show and how long each stays up. The display fades between them with a slow, ghostly dissolve — each board keeps its own layout.

On the bar screen: open the link, then press F11 for fullscreen. Press P on that screen to pause/resume the rotation.

❖ Promotions — Members' Prices

Prices live in a Google Sheet, like the other boards. Edit them there and the Promotions board updates within a few minutes.

▦ Preview the board

First-time setup: make a Google Sheet with a tab named prices and a header row Name · Price · Price2 · Note (Price2 and Note optional). Share it “Anyone with the link – Viewer”, then paste its ID into PRICES_SHEET_ID in both settings.html and board.html.
